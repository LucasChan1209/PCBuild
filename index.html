About Me

My name is Lucas I am a PC Builder from Hong Kong. I’ve been active in the extreme pc building since 2007 and within this time I have always created and successfully used all kinds of tools, among them LN2/Dice containers, Direct Die Frames, and delidding tools.

Here I also offer a wide range of Gaming-PCs, my OC-Tools and Merchandise.

Furthermore, I am involved as an CEO with Cool Workshop.

When i am not working on PCs and hardware stuff, I enjoy outdoor activities. You can also find me at Facebook or Twitter.
